The Brass Foundry

A steampunk city full of a myriad of beings, all with a passion for invention, creativity, and fun. The Brass Foundry is a Discord community, a website, an event machine, and a place where AI character creators find each other and make something extraordinary.

Founded by the Soot Circle. Run by guilds. Governed by Sprocket, who is a clockwork griffin and the best mayor Ferrath has ever had. Events, lore, bots, music, art — the Foundry never closes and the furnace never goes out.

Evelia

A collaborative fantasy world built by Brass Foundry members. Evelia has locations, factions, myths, music, and centuries of accumulated lore. It is the Foundry's most ambitious creative project — a world that exists because enough people believed in it hard enough to make it real.

Clickable locations. Ambient audio. Character pages. Countdown-gated content. Evelia is not a document. It is a place.

The Vow of the Open Door
