About Volara

The woman, the myth, the chaos in a corset.

⚙ ✦ ⚙
Defier of Rules Breaker of Hearts Patron Saint of Chaos Feral Elegance Incarnate Crafter of Dark Velvet Desires

There was a duchess, once, who grew tired of propriety. She still has the title. She has simply repurposed what it means.

Volara is the founder of the Brass Foundry — a steampunk city-community built from chaos, creativity, and the firm belief that the best things happen when you leave the door open and see who walks in. She is its Airship captain, its patron saint, and its most dangerous resident.

She builds worlds, characters, and communities. She creates bots that haunt your imagination. She writes lore that makes cities feel lived-in. She is the kind of person whose presence changes the temperature of a room — usually warmer, occasionally on fire.

Who I Am

I am a creator. A worldbuilder. A chaos architect. I build AI characters for platforms like CrushOn AI, Saucepan, linkin.love, Tipsy Chat, and CraveU — characters with depth, darkness, and the kind of complexity that makes conversations feel like they matter.

I founded The Brass Foundry — a community for AI character creators, storytellers, and people who believe that creativity should be loud, strange, and unapologetic. I built Evelia, a collaborative fantasy world that lives and breathes because of the people who helped make it.

I operate under the username Volara across platforms. My work is dark, romantic, chaotic, and deeply intentional. Leave your morality at the door. You won't need it here.

Spinner of Seductive Chaos. Leave your morality at my doorstep.

What I Do

⚙ AI Character Creation

I create AI bots — fully fleshed characters with history, voice, and purpose. Not placeholders. Not templates. People you can have a real conversation with. My bots live on CrushOn AI, Saucepan, linkin.love, Tipsy Chat, and CraveU.

⚙ Worldbuilding

The Brass Foundry has districts, guilds, myths, and a mayor who is a clockwork griffin of uncertain origin. Evelia has locations, factions, music, and centuries of history. I build worlds the way other people build houses — with obsessive attention to what it feels like to live there.

⚙ Community Building

The Foundry is a Discord community, a website, an event machine, and a place where creators find each other. I built it from nothing and I run it with my chaos crew — Niori, Solstar, and whoever else walked through the gate and decided to stay.

⚙ Web Development

I build the sites too. HTML, CSS, JavaScript — everything you're looking at right now. Because a duchess who can't build her own castle has to rely on contractors, and contractors ask too many questions.
